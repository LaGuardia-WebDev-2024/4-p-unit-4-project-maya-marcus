My Unofficial Local Guide to My Neighborhood (UES)

The Met/Central Park

Get the very iconic Gossip Girl experience! Since the Met and Central Park are right near each other you can walk around both of them! Especially in the fall the park is so pretty.

  • Take pictures on the Met steps
  • Look at all of the paintings
  • Walk around the park and see the fall colors everywhere



Shopping on Madison Avenue

You can find every store you could think of here. It's always filled with people and it's very lively.

  • Every designer store
  • High End Fashion
  • Art Galleries and Resturants

Anita Gelato

The BEST ice cream you will ever have!

  • Delicious and unique flavors
